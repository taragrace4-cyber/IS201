More about Hungary!

  1. The Hungarian Parliament Building is a landmark
  2. Hungary is known for its thermal baths and natural hot springs
    • Budapest is often called the “City of Spas” because of its numerous thermal baths.
    • The Széchenyi Thermal Bath is one of the largest medicinal baths in Europe.
    • Thermal waters are believed to have healing properties and are popular for relaxation.
  3. Hungary has a rich cultural and musical heritage
    • Birthplace of famous composers like Franz Liszt and Béla Bartók.
    • Folk music and dance traditions are still celebrated across the country.
    • Traditional festivals, like the Busójárás in Mohács, showcase colorful masks and local customs.
